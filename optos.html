Upgrades to Technology to Better Serve Our Patients

Optomap Retinal Scan

Bieter Eye Center has UPGRADED the technology we use to take a digital scan of the back of your eye. It will enhance the eye exam, as it will help further evaluate the overall health of your eye, and potentially detect systemic eye diseases such as diabetes, glaucoma, macular degeneration, and even cancer.

It will also identify if there is a presence of any retinal lesions, such as holes, tears or detachments, which can present without any signs or symptoms.

EPIC Refraction System

Bieter Eye Center is proud and excited to announce the new technology we now have in our office called the EPIC Refraction System. The EPIC is a computerized system that very precisely allows the glasses prescription to be determined electronically from a keypad. It is quicker and easier than with the traditional method.

We love to be able to offer the most advanced and accurate eye exam possible.
