Why Use Our Optical at Bieter Eye Center?

Quality: We use the latest and best lenses with the technological advancements to protect your eyes and ensure the best vision possible. We also utilize the Essilor Visioffice® technology, which allows us to obtain every possible measurement required for modern lenses with extreme accuracy. In addition, we have our own in-office lab which allows us to be in better control of the quality of your glasses.

Warranty: We take the worry out of your glasses. We offer a 1-year warranty against broken frames and scratched lenses. We also will perform any frame adjustments or repairs, replace nose pads, and replace temple covers at no cost!

Value: Many times we can offer a lower cost than some of the chains. We try to offer a wide range of products to accommodate all budgets. We hope the trust you have gained in our ability as your optometrist will be carried through to your eyewear needs also.

Frame Selection: We stay on top of styles when it comes to the frames in our optical. If there is a frame we don't have or a different size/color you may want, we are happy to order in something else to help you make your decision. If for some reason you decide you are unhappy with the frame style you have purchased, we will work with you to re-select a new frame at no charge. We truly want you to be happy with your glasses!

Lens Options: We pride ourselves in keeping up with ever-changing lens technology. We can provide you with lighter and thinner lenses that are glare free. We can also offer the latest advancements in progressive no-line bifocals, no fog lenses, and in Transitions® which turn from light to dark in the sun. There are even Transitions® that become polarized when they turn dark!

Expert Staff: Our staff is well trained to be able to professionally meet your needs. We will do our best to get it right the first time from start to finish. We are also trained to help with any problems that may come up along the way, such as difficulty adjusting to a prescription. We truly care about our patients. You are not just buying glasses, but our service as well. If you have any problems that arise we are always here to help; we'll do whatever it takes!

Last but not least, THANK YOU for trusting us and allowing us to give you complete eye exams and great eyewear that hopefully lets you have the best vision possible! You, our patients, are the reason we are here!
